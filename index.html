About KPS Barbecue

KPS Barbecue is a community group dedicated to compiling local barbecue events into one, easy to use tool. The service is meant for people that want to hang out, meet new people, and have a good barbecue! Event organizers can have their events uploaded to the site in order to spread the word and get people excited about barbecue.
